Healthy Dorm Snacks

Discover easy and nutritious snacks that you can prepare in your dorm room.

Snacks You Can Make in 5 Minutes

  • Fruit and Nut Mix: Combine nuts, seeds, and dried fruits for a healthy snack.
  • Peanut Butter & Banana: Spread peanut butter on a banana for a quick and energy-boosting snack.

Healthy Dorm-Friendly Snacks

  • Greek Yogurt with Honey: Top Greek yogurt with honey and granola for a satisfying snack.
  • Veggie Sticks with Hummus: Fresh carrot and cucumber sticks paired with hummus.
